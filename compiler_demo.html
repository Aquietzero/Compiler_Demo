Instructions:

There are some rules you have to obey while playing here. I have made some default settings while creating the site. They are listed up above where they are suppose to be entered.

Terminals

  • The "e" is used to represent the "ɛ" in Dragon Book.
  • The "$" is still valid in this site to represent the right endmarker.
  • The terminals should be enter one by one, separated by "," followed with a whitespace. For example: +, *, id.

Grammar

  • Productions with different head should be listed in different line.
  • Productions with the same head can be listed in the same line, different bodies of the productions are separated by a " | ". Please pay attention to the whitespaces around the "|"
  • Terminals or nonterminals in the production body should be separated with whitespace.
